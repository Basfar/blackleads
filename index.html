Why

In our quest to ensure that young persons in Africa are well equipped going into the future, we discovered a gap between the priviledged and the underserved in the areas of leadership and exposure across various areas. We therefore seek to bridge this gap by helping young students become better versions of themselves in today’s thoroughly competitive world.

How

We believe leadership and exposure are encompassing fundamental qualities that must be instilled in every child from a young age. It is in this light we organize practical leadership workshops and exposure opportunities through sustainable partnerships that impact on these young students.
